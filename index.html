Menstrual equity through awareness, access, and dignity.

Campaign Shakti is a student-led nonprofit working to break menstrual stigma and increase access to sanitary products for girls and women in underserved communities in India.

Why It Matters

Millions of girls in India lack access to safe menstrual products and education, leading to health risks, missed school, and deep-rooted shame.

What We Do

We raise funds and partner with trusted organizations in India to distribute menstrual hygiene products and support education initiatives.
